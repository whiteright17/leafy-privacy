Privacy Policy

App: Leafy – Plant Care & Watering  |  Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Leafy ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to handling your data responsibly and transparently.

1. Information We Collect

Data type What exactly Why Linked to you?
Precise Location GPS coordinates at the moment of a weather request To fetch local weather and adjust your watering schedule automatically No
Photos Photos you choose to scan for plant identification Sent to an AI service to recognize the plant species No
Other User Content Messages you send to the AI Botanist chat Processed by an AI service to generate care advice No
Purchase History Subscription status (free / Pro) To unlock Pro features via RevenueCat No
Device ID Anonymous device-level identifier Analytics (Firebase, TelemetryDeck) No
Product Interaction Taps, screen views, feature usage To understand which features are most useful and improve the app No
Crash Data Crash logs and stack traces To detect and fix bugs (Firebase Crashlytics) No

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other contact information. We do not collect health data or financial information beyond subscription status.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • App Functionality – Location is used solely to retrieve local weather; photos and chat messages are sent to AI services only to produce the response and are not stored by us.
  • Subscription management – Purchase history is processed by RevenueCat to determine whether your Pro subscription is active.
  • Analytics & improvement – Anonymous usage data and device identifiers help us understand how the app is used so we can add features and fix issues.
  • Crash reporting – Crash logs are used exclusively to identify and resolve technical problems.

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party SDKs and services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (Google LLC) – anonymous usage and crash data. Firebase Privacy
  • RevenueCat – subscription and purchase management. RevenueCat Privacy
  • TelemetryDeck – privacy-focused, hash-anonymised analytics. TelemetryDeck Privacy
  • Weather API – your coordinates are sent to retrieve current weather; they are not stored beyond the duration of the request.
  • AI services – photos and chat messages are transmitted to process your request; we do not store them on our servers.

4. Your Plant Data & iCloud

All data you enter into the app – plant names, watering logs, diary notes, growth photos – is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via your personal iCloud account (Apple's CloudKit). This data is accessible only to you and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We never have access to your iCloud data.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain personal data on our own servers. Analytics data retained by Firebase and TelemetryDeck is governed by their respective policies. Crash logs are automatically deleted after 90 days by Firebase.

6. Children's Privacy

Leafy is rated 9+ on the App Store and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete data associated with you. Since we do not collect identifiable personal information, most requests can be fulfilled by deleting the app from your device, which removes all locally stored data. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at the address below.
