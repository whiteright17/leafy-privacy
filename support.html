Frequently Asked Questions

How does Leafy build my watering schedule?

Leafy combines your plant's species, its light level and pot, and your local weather and season to recommend when to water and feed — so you avoid over- and under-watering.

I subscribed to Pro but don't see the features.

Open Profile → Restore Purchases. Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID, so this re-syncs it to your device. Make sure you're signed in with the Apple ID used for the purchase.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your device, or apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions. You can cancel anytime; access continues until the end of the paid period.

Does my data sync across devices?

Yes. Your plants, notes and photos sync through your personal iCloud account. Make sure iCloud is enabled on each device with the same Apple ID.

How do I identify a plant?

Tap the camera / scan option and take a clear photo of the plant. Leafy uses AI to recognize the species and suggest care.
